# Chapter 5 Mechanical Vibration 机械振动

## 核心公式速查

| Concept | Formula | Notes/Conditions |
|---------|---------|------------------|
| SHM Equation | `x = A*cos(omega*t + phi)` | omega = sqrt(k/m) for spring oscillator |
| Velocity & Acceleration | `v = -omega*A*sin(omega*t+phi), a = -omega^2*x` | v leads x by pi/2; a is opposite to x |
| Period & Frequency | `T = 2*pi/omega, f = 1/T` | Spring: T = 2*pi*sqrt(m/k); Pendulum: T = 2*pi*sqrt(l/g) |
| SHM Energy | `E = k*A^2/2 = m*v^2/2 + k*x^2/2` | Total energy conserved, proportional to A^2 |
| Superposition (same direction) | `A = sqrt(A1^2+A2^2+2*A1*A2*cos(Delta_phi))` | Delta_phi=2k*pi constructive, (2k+1)*pi destructive |
| Beat Frequency | `f_beat = |f1 - f2|` | When two frequencies are close |
| Damped Vibration | `Amplitude decays exponentially` | Under/critical/over damping |
| Forced Vibration & Resonance | `Steady-state frequency = driving frequency` | Resonance when driving_freq ~ natural_freq |
