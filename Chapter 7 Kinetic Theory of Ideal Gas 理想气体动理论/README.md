# Chapter 7 Kinetic Theory of Ideal Gas 理想气体动理论

## 核心公式速查

| Concept | Formula | Notes/Conditions |
|---------|---------|------------------|
| Ideal Gas Law | `p*V = nu*R*T` | p*V = N*k*T, R = N_A*k = 8.31 J/(mol*K) |
| Pressure (Microscopic) | `p = n*m0*<v^2>/3` | Statistical average of molecular collisions on walls |
| Temperature & Kinetic Energy | `m0*<v^2>/2 = 3*k*T/2` | Temperature measures average translational KE of molecules |
| Degrees of Freedom i | `i = t + r + 2s` | Monatomic=3, Rigid diatomic=5, Non-rigid diatomic=7 |
| Equipartition Theorem | `Average KE per DOF = k*T/2` | Energy equally distributed among all DOFs at equilibrium |
| Internal Energy of Ideal Gas | `E = i*nu*R*T/2` | Depends ONLY on temperature and amount of substance |
| Maxwell Speed Distribution | `f(v) = 4*pi*(m0/2*pi*k*T)^(3/2) * v^2 * exp(-m0*v^2/2kT)` | Probability density for molecular speeds |
| Three Characteristic Speeds | `v_p < v_avg < v_rms` | v_p=sqrt(2RT/M), v_avg=sqrt(8RT/pi*M), v_rms=sqrt(3RT/M) |
| Mean Free Path | `lambda_bar = 1/(sqrt(2)*n*sigma)` | lambda_bar = kT/(sqrt(2)*pi*d^2*p), inversely proportional to p |
| Collision Frequency | `Z_bar = v_bar/lambda_bar` | Average collisions per unit time |
